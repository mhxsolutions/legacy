How to answers Rick's questions

Driver’s Revenue by Day includes revenue per planned minute with planned throughput and payroll information.

Planned Load Revenue by Driver also has planned throughput with the ability to see the detail breakout.

 

Planned Load Revenue Detail shows billing calculations for a given day’s loads. Filtered by terminal.

Invoice Information by Date shows load invoices based on load date. It’s not a perfect report to match a month’s revenue numbers, but it does allow you to audit the invoice (not billing) attached to each load.

 

Planned Minutes by Month which has totals and terminal breakdowns. This report also shows planned throughput.

O/D pair Planned Times can be used to audit the transit and dwell times used by the planner.
